## Recent Updates

<Callout kind="info">
  Check this changelog regularly to stay informed about new programs, application updates, and platform enhancements for the Pakaims platform. You receive notifications via email when you enable them in your account settings.
</Callout>

Pakaims evolves rapidly to support IMS's commitment to innovation. You find details on platform enhancements, new programs, streamlined applications, bug fixes, and performance boosts below. Review the tagged releases to prioritize updates relevant to your needs.

<Update label="2024-10-15" description="v1.2.0" tags={["feature", "improvement"]}>
## New Features

- Added Artificial Intelligence undergraduate program with dedicated application portal
- Introduced real-time application status tracking dashboard
- Enhanced program search with filters for duration, fees, and affiliation

## Improvements

- Optimized load times for program listings by `>40%`
- Updated faculty profiles with research publications and industry experience

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed application form submission errors on mobile devices
- Resolved search filters not persisting across page refreshes
</Update>
