## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

- A valid email address
- Internet access
- Basic information ready: name, date of birth, academic history

<Callout kind="info">
Pakaims supports major browsers like Chrome, Firefox, and Safari. Disable ad blockers for the best experience.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to sign up on Pakaims.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Portal" icon="globe">
    Go to `https://pakaims.com/signup`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Registration Form">
    Enter your email, create a password, and provide basic details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    Check your inbox for the verification link from `no-reply@pakaims.com` and click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile">
    Log in and update your profile with academic qualifications.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Navigate the Student Portal

Once logged in at `https://dashboard.pakaims.com`, use the main navigation.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Dashboard" icon="home">
    View your overview, upcoming deadlines, and quick actions.

    - Profile summary
    - Application status
    - Notifications
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Programs" icon="book-open">
    Browse undergraduate and graduate offerings.

    Use the search bar to filter by discipline like `Computer Science` or `Business Administration`.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Applications" icon="file-text">
    Track submissions and requirements.

    Upload documents here for review.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Submit Initial Application Details

Start your application process securely.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Program" icon="search">
    Choose from explored programs and click `Apply Now`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Provide personal info, transcripts, and references.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Submit">
    Preview your form, then submit. You'll receive a confirmation email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Save your application ID for tracking. Applications are reviewed within `>7 days`.
</Callout>
