---
title: Can't log in to the IMS Online Portal
description: Quick fix for login problems with the IMS portal.
---

## Problems

- Wrong password error
- Login page not loading

## Causes

- Forgotten/incorrect password
- Browser cache issues

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Check your email for reset instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Refresh and clear your browser cache. Try logging in again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
