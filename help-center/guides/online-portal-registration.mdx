## Quick Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open registration page">
    Go to `https://portal.pakaims.edu.pk/register`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in details">
    Enter your name, student ID, email, and create a password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify email">
    Check your email for a verification link. Click to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Use your credentials to log in at `https://portal.pakaims.edu.pk`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
