title: How do I apply for admission at IMS?
description: Admission process for IMS.
---

## Steps to Apply

1. Visit the admissions page at `https://pakaims.edu.pk/admissions`
2. Complete the online application form
3. Upload required documents (transcripts, certificates)
4. Pay the application fee online or at the campus
5. Submit your application

## After Submission

- Check your email for application status and test/interview schedule

## Need Help?

Email admissions@pakaims.edu.pk for assistance.
