## Overview

Pakaims provides access to the Institute of Management Sciences (IMS), where you pursue high-quality education in management and computer sciences. Established in 1986, IMS offers undergraduate and graduate programs affiliated with leading international universities. You benefit from industry connections, research opportunities, and a focus on innovation.

<Callout kind="info">
IMS emphasizes practical skills through modern labs, expert faculty, and real-world projects.
</Callout>

### Program Overview

| Program Level | Discipline | Duration | Key Focus Areas |
|---------------|------------|----------|-----------------|
| Undergraduate | Business Administration | 4 years | Marketing, Finance, Operations |
| Undergraduate | Computer Science | 4 years | Programming, Databases, Networks |
| Graduate | Computer Science | 2 years | Algorithms, Software Engineering |
| Graduate | Artificial Intelligence | 2 years | Machine Learning, Data Science, Ethics |
